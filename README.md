There are a thousand "best AI tools" lists. Most are affiliate-link dumps written by people who never sat an exam. This one is different: every tool here is picked for one question — **does it help a student understand more, faster, without outsourcing the actual learning?** Tools that just write your essay for you are *not* the goal. Tools that quiz you, explain hard ideas, summarize 40 papers, or free up the hours you waste on busywork — those are.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> AI is a **tutor and a power tool, not a ghostwriter.** Used well it makes you sharper. Used lazily it makes you dependent and gets you caught. Read [How to use AI without cheating yourself](#-how-to-use-ai-without-cheating-yourself) first — it's the most important section here.

If this saved you time, **⭐ star the repo** so other students find it, and **[contribute](CONTRIBUTING.md)** the tool that saved *you*.

## 🧭 How to use AI without cheating yourself

The students who win with AI treat it like a **personal tutor available 24/7**, not a vending machine for answers. Seven habits that separate learning from cheating:

1. **Make it ask *you* questions.** "Quiz me on this chapter, one question at a time, and don't show the answer until I try." Active recall beats re-reading every time.
2. **Use it to understand, then produce the work yourself.** Ask it to *explain* a concept five ways; write the essay in your own words. The explanation is the value; the output is yours.
3. **Always verify.** LLMs hallucinate confident nonsense — fake citations, wrong dates, invented formulas. Cross-check anything that matters against a primary source.
4. **Know your school's policy.** Rules differ by course and country. "I didn't know it counted as misconduct" is not a defense. When unsure, ask your instructor *before*, not after.
5. **Never paste private or graded material into tools you don't trust.** Assume free tools may train on your input. Prefer 🔒 privacy-friendly or local options for sensitive work.
6. **Keep your fingerprints on the thinking.** AI-detectors are unreliable, but originality is real: your argument, your examples, your voice. Use AI for scaffolding, not soul.
7. **Protect the skill you're there to build.** If a tool removes the exact struggle that *is* the learning (doing the proof, writing the code, forming the argument), use it *after* you've tried, not instead of trying.

> A good test: **"If my professor watched me use this, would I be proud or embarrassed?"** Build the habit now — it's the same habit that separates good professionals from replaceable ones.

## 🧠 General AI assistants
Your everyday "explain it, summarize it, quiz me" companions.

- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) — The default all-rounder for explanations, summaries, and brainstorming. 🆓 ⭐
- [Claude](https://claude.ai) — Strong at long documents, careful reasoning, and nuanced writing feedback. 🆓 ⭐
- [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) — Tightly integrated with Google Docs, Gmail, and search. 🆓 🎓
- [Microsoft Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com) — Free GPT-class chat plus Office integration; uses Bing for fresh sources. 🆓
- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai) — Answer engine that **cites its sources** — far safer for facts than a raw chatbot. 🆓 🎓 ⭐
- [Le Chat (Mistral)](https://chat.mistral.ai) — Fast, capable European assistant with a generous free tier. 🆓

## ✍️ Writing & editing
Fix grammar, tighten arguments, learn to write better — without letting it write *for* you.

- [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com) — Grammar, clarity, and tone feedback as you type. 🆓 🎓 ⭐
- [QuillBot](https://quillbot.com) — Paraphraser, grammar checker, and citation generator in one. 🆓 🎓
- [LanguageTool](https://languagetool.org) — Open-source grammar/style checker in 30+ languages; self-hostable. 🆓 🔒
- [Hemingway Editor](https://hemingwayapp.com) — Flags dense, passive, hard-to-read sentences so you write clearer. 🆓
- [Wordtune](https://www.wordtune.com) — Rewrites and rephrases your sentences while keeping your meaning. 🆓

## 🔬 Research & reading papers
Find, summarize, and interrogate the literature — the biggest time-saver in higher ed.

- [Google NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com) — Upload your sources; it answers **grounded only in them**, with citations and audio overviews. 🆓 ⭐
- [Elicit](https://elicit.com) — Search 125M+ papers, extract data into tables, run systematic-review-style screening. 🆓 ⭐
- [Consensus](https://consensus.app) — Ask a yes/no research question; see what peer-reviewed papers actually conclude. 🆓
- [SciSpace](https://scispace.com) — All-in-one: literature search, "explain this paper" reader, reference library. 🆓
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org) — Free academic search engine with AI summaries (TLDRs) and citation graphs. 🆓 🔒
- [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com) — Visual graph of how papers relate — perfect for mapping a new field. 🆓
- [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org) — The gold-standard free reference manager; AI plugins available. 🆓 🔒 ⭐

## 📒 Notes & knowledge management
Capture, organize, and resurface what you learn.

- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) — Local-first Markdown notes with backlinks and an AI plugin ecosystem; your data stays yours. 🆓 🔒 ⭐
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) — All-in-one docs/databases with built-in Notion AI for summaries and Q&A. 🆓 🎓
- [Google NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com) — Doubles as a "chat with my lecture notes" knowledge base. 🆓
- [Otter.ai](https://otter.ai) — Records and transcribes lectures in real time, then summarizes them. 🆓 🎓

## 🎯 Studying, flashcards & memory
Active recall + spaced repetition is the most evidence-backed way to remember anything.

- [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net) — The legendary free, open-source spaced-repetition flashcard system. 🆓 🔒 ⭐
- [Quizlet](https://quizlet.com) — Flashcards, practice tests, and an AI tutor (Q-Chat). 🆓 🎓
- [RemNote](https://www.remnote.com) — Note-taking with spaced repetition built in — turn notes into flashcards automatically. 🆓
- [Khanmigo](https://www.khanmigo.ai) — Khan Academy's AI tutor that guides you to the answer instead of handing it over. 🎓 ⭐
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) — Free world-class courses across math, science, and the humanities. 🆓

## 🧮 Math, science & data
Solve, *and understand how it was solved.*

- [Wolfram Alpha](https://www.wolframalpha.com) — Computational engine for math, physics, chemistry — with step-by-step solutions. 🆓 🎓 ⭐
- [Photomath](https://photomath.com) — Snap a photo of a math problem; get worked, step-by-step solutions. 🆓
- [Symbolab](https://www.symbolab.com) — Step-by-step solver for algebra, calculus, and more. 🆓
- [Julius AI](https://julius.ai) — Chat with your data: upload CSV/Excel/SPSS and get analysis and charts, no code. 🆓
- [Desmos](https://www.desmos.com) — Free graphing/scientific calculators that make functions visual and intuitive. 🆓 🔒

## 💻 Coding & CS learning
Learn to build — and let AI explain code instead of just spitting it out.

- [GitHub Copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot) — AI pair-programmer in your editor; **free for verified students** via the Student Pack. 🆓 🎓 ⭐
- [Cursor](https://cursor.com) — AI-native code editor; great "explain this codebase" and learn-by-building flow. 🆓
- [Replit](https://replit.com) — Code in the browser with an AI assistant — zero setup, perfect for beginners. 🆓 🎓
- [Phind](https://www.phind.com) — Search engine for developers that answers with sources and code. 🆓
- [Exercism](https://exercism.org) — Free coding practice in 70+ languages with human mentorship. 🆓 🔒

- [Otter.ai](https://otter.ai) — Live captions and transcripts for lectures. 🆓 🎓

## 🔒 Run AI free & private (local)
No subscription, no data leaving your laptop. Most run on a modest machine — no GPU required for small models.

- [Ollama](https://ollama.com) — The simplest way to run open models (Llama, Mistral, Phi, Gemma) locally. 🆓 🔒 ⭐
- [LM Studio](https://lmstudio.ai) — Friendly desktop app to download and chat with local models. 🆓 🔒
- [Jan](https://jan.ai) — Open-source, offline ChatGPT alternative. 🆓 🔒
- [GPT4All](https://www.nomic.ai/gpt4all) — Run private chatbots on everyday hardware. 🆓 🔒
- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co) — The home of open models, datasets, and free learning resources. 🆓
